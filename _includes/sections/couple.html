Pablo y Liza

Pablo Rengifo, nativo de Cali, y Liza Gruesso, nativa de Bogotá, se conocieron en Guasca en el 2016 gracias a su amiga PepaPig.

It didn't take long for Pablo and Liza to realize that they had a lot in common. They quickly bonded over a love of cheesy nineties songs, brunch, Russian, and dumb jokes (okay, that last one is really just Pablo). Soon they were spending every minute together.

In early 2017, Pablo and Liza moved in together, and Mr. Paul joined the family.

Pablo and Liza both left KU in 2016 and now work in Bogota. Pablo is an manager for Banco de Alimentos, where he helps ensure lower income families and individuals are fed. Liza is an independent consultant at Micro & Macro, a Bogotá consultancy focused on public service contracts.

Pablo and Liza both love their jobs and are looking forward to starting the next chapter of their lives together in Bogotá.
